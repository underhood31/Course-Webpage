About Our Institute

Indraprastha Institute of Information Technology, Delhi (aka. IIIT-Delhi or IIIT-D) was created as a State University by an act of Govt. of NCT of Delhi (The IIIT Delhi Act, 2007) empowering it to do research and development, and grant degrees. In a relatively short time, it has earned an excellent reputation in India and abroad for being a center of quality education and research in IT and interdisciplinary areas.Know More

Grow Your Career

IIIT-Delhi is an exciting place to lay a strong foundation for a long and enriching global career.

  • Highly prolific faculty with about 2/3rd having Overseas research and work experience
  • Overseas Research Fellowships for Ph.D. students
  • Industry Sponsored Labs and Centres
  • X-disciplinary and Flexible Curriculum
  • Collaborative M.Tech. Thesis with Industry
  • Industry Facing Courses
  • Financial Support to participate in Conferences
  • On-campus Incubation Center
  • Special Start-up career fairs
  • More than 12% of IIIT-Delhi alumni are presently pursuing higher studies abroad.

Infrastructure and Facilities

IIIT-Delhi sports world class infrastructure to enable great learning and all-rounded development of students

  • Connected to Delhi from 3 metro stations on different sides of campus
  • Smart Classrooms of varying capacities - 500 to 25 students
  • Scalable High Performance Computing Lab
  • Modern, well furnished hostels with Central Air Conditioning
  • High Speed Wifi enabled Campus
  • State-of-the-art Sports Complex with Indoor Swimming Pool & Fully equipped Gymnasium
  • Richly appointed Library Complex with Collaborative workspaces
  • Studios for married students
  • 24hrs open Cafeteria
  • IIIT-Delhi has been consistently ranked amongst the top 5 Clean Campuses in Technical Residential Category for the past 2 years.
